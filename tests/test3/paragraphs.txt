The following is a table of marks for the first year for the course of Natural Language Processing. It consists of both internal marks and final marks.
The internal marks are the marks that are awarded for the digital assignment (DA) and the two quizes along with the two CAT assessments.
CAT means Continous Assessment Test. FAT means Final Assessment Test.
The marks for CAT 1 and CAT 2 are out of 50. The marks for FAT is out of 100. Both quizes, Quiz 1 and Quiz 2 are scored out of 10. The Digital Assignment is also scored out of 10.
